Death Note

The story of Light Yagami, a brilliant but bored high school student who discovers a mysterious notebook called the Death Note. This notebook gives him the power to kill anyone simply by writing their name in it while picturing their face. Believing he can make the world a better place, Light uses the notebook to eliminate criminals and create a new world where he rules as a god. However, his actions draw the attention of a genius detective known as L, leading to a thrilling battle of intelligence and morality. The anime explores deep questions about justice, power, and the consequences of playing god.
